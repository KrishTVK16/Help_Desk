Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account, raise a ticket, or communicate with our support team. This includes your name, email address, company name, and technical details regarding your support requests.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide, maintain, and improve our services. We use technical data from support tickets to train our AI support cores, ensuring faster response times for all users. Your personal data is never sold to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SOC 2 Type II compliance and full end-to-end encryption for all support communications.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time via your user dashboard settings.
